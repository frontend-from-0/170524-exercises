About Cat's

What breed of cat is the prettiest?
In the study, where the golden ratio of "1.62" was used, the Norwegian forest cat, Russian Blue, Manx, Angora cat and Van cat were the most beautiful cats. In contrast, the Himalayan, Peterbald, and Persian cat were found to be the least mathematically beautiful.
What breed of cat is the cutest?
Another breed that deserves the title of "the most beautiful cat in the world" is the Bengal cat. By temperament, they are sweet, affectionate, athletic and curious. Bengal originated after the crossbreeding of the Asian leopard cat with a domestic cat.
What breed of cat doesn't shed?
The only cat breed that can be defined as a cat that does not shed hair is the breed known as Sphynx or Sphinx, which is famous for its lack of hair.
